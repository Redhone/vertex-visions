ABOUT ME

As a passionate 22-year-old architect specializing in 3D design, I blend creativity with technical expertise to bring architectural visions to life. My journey in architecture began with a fascination for structures and spatial design, leading me to pursue a degree where I honed my skills in both traditional and cutting-edge techniques. With a keen eye for detail and a love for innovation, I focus on creating immersive, dynamic 3D models that capture the essence of each project. My work spans residential, commercial, and conceptual design, allowing me to explore diverse architectural styles and solutions.
