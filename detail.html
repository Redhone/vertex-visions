PROJECT DETAILS

This 3D modeling project focuses on creating a detailed and realistic digital representation of a modern villa. The design captures the essence of luxury living, featuring a spacious layout with multiple levels, expansive glass windows, and sleek architectural lines. The villa includes an open-plan living area, gourmet kitchen, and several bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms. Outdoor spaces are highlighted by a swimming pool, landscaped gardens, and a patio for entertaining. The model showcases various materials, textures, and lighting to enhance realism, offering a comprehensive visual experience. This project is ideal for architectural visualization, client presentations, or virtual tours.
